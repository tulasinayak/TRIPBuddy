## 🗺️ Itinerary

 **DAY 1: Exploring Historic Berlin**

*Morning:*
Begin your day with a visit to the iconic Brandenburg Gate, symbolizing peace and unity. Afterward, take a short walk (5 min) to the nearby Victory Column, offering panoramic views of the city from its observation deck (free admission).

*Afternoon:*
Head over to Museum Island (€18), home to five museums dedicated to various periods in history and art. Spend around 2-3 hours exploring these architectural masterpieces at your leisure. A 5-minute walk from Museum Island brings you to the Berlin Cathedral (€7), a stunning architectural gem worth visiting.

*Hidden Gem Tip:* Discover the serene Garden of the Academies, a beautiful oasis hidden between museums on Museum Island. Admission is free, and it's the perfect spot for relaxation amidst your sightseeing.

*Evening:*
Explore East Side Gallery (free), an iconic 1.3 km section of the Berlin Wall showcasing outdoor artwork by artists from around the world. Afterward, enjoy a leisurely stroll along the Spree River promenade, where you can find various food vendors and street performers to unwind and reflect on your day's adventures.

*Total estimated spend for activities:* Approx €25 (€18 Museum Island + €7 Berlin Cathedral)
